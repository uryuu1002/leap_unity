Process | Over View of Leap Motion

LEAP MOTION is a computer hardware sensor device that “supports hand and

finger motions as input, analogous to a mouse, but requires no hand

contact or touching.”

This video clip shows my working process of my final project when I first

accessed Unity and Leap Motion at the same time.Through online tutorials

and the premade Unity Leap Motioncore assets, I was able to create simple

objects and environmentsthat allows my hand to be detected and interacted

with my object creations. Rigidbody, gravity, collision components, along

with interactive behavior script, my hands were able to interact with the

virtual world within Unity.
